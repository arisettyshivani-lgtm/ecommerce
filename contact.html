Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to select countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. International orders may be subject to customs fees.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unused items in original packaging. Custom or personalized items cannot be returned unless defective.

Are your products handmade?

Yes, all our products are handcrafted by skilled artisans. Each piece may have slight variations that reflect the handmade nature and add to its unique character.

How do I care for my purchase?

Care instructions are included with each product. Generally, we recommend hand washing ceramics, spot cleaning textiles, and treating wood with food-safe oil periodically.
